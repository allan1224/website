Brain Stimulation Headband System Design

Trasncranial photobiomodulation can increase cognitive funciton and promote a healthy brain. Previous research has shown the effect of 1064 nm light on brain waves, corresponding to improved congitive funciton. Part of my job as a lab technician at the Gonzaelz-lima Lab is to analyze how a 1064 nm laser wtih a power density of 250 mW/cm^2 affects EEG signals. In the figure below are topo plots I have made measuring the change in mean band powers between stimulation and placebo (no laser stimulation), just to demonstrate that infrared light at this frequency can effect neural oscillations.
