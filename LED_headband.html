Brain Stimulation Headband System Design

Trasncranial photobiomodulation can increase cognitive funciton and promote a healthy brain. Previous research has shown the effect of 1064 nm light on brain waves, corresponding to improved congitive funciton. Part of my job as a lab technician at the Gonzaelz-lima Lab is to analyze how a 1064 nm laser wtih a power density of 250 mW/cm^2 affects EEG signals. However, this laser is extermely expensive and incovneinent for most people to actually use on a daily basis - we can't all have $20k lasers in our homes.

 

Just to provide some motivation behind this project, below are some topo plots I made measuring the change in mean band powers between stimulation and placebo (no laser stimulation) - the laser correspoonds to an increase in mean alpha power, and a decrease in mean beta and delta powers.
