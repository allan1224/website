Brain Stimulation Headband

A non-invase neuromodulation device to improve cognitive function using infrared LEDs

 
 
 

Photobiomodulation is the application of near-infrared light to promote healing, decrease pain, and restore cellular energy balance. When this light shines on the brain, it can improve cognitive and emotional function, having a wide variety of application. This is usually done with an expensive laser that requires special training, making it anything but conveneint for most people to frequently use. This project is an attempt to provide a low cost, alternative, portable, and safe light therapy device.
