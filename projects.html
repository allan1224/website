Brain Stimulation Headband

A non-invase neuromodulation device to improve cognitive function using infrared LEDs (1050-1070 nm)

 
 

Photobiomodulation is the application of near-infrared light to promote healing, decrease pain, and restore cellular energy balance. Transcranial Photobiomodulation (aimed at the forhead) has been shown to improve cognitive and emotional functions in humans, which has a wide variety of use cases. This is usually done with a very expensive laser that requires special training, making it anything but conveneint for most people to frequently use. This project is an attempt to provide a low cost, alternative, portable, and safe light therapy device that hopefully people can easily use one day.
